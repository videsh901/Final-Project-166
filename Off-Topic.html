What Is Bitcoin?: The Future of Money Possibly





Bitcoin is a form of digital money or cryptocurrency. Bitcoin is mine by special computers that solve complicated math problems. Anyone can use Bitcoin as long as they have a computer or a smartphone. Bitcoin can be store on hot wallet (software) or a cold storage wallet (hardware). These wallets have programs that allow for storing, sending, and receiving Bitcoin. You can send Bitcoin to any location in the world. Bitcoin's price is determined by the supply and demand for it. Bitcoin is a decentralized which means no one controls it. There will only ever be 21 million supply in it's entire. Some says Bitcoin will be the future of currency but others call it out as a ponzi scheme. To learn more about Bitcoin check out this website.

The Fermi's Paradox: Where Are The Aliens?





The Fermi's Paradox asks the question that if they are billions of planets in the universe then how come we have not discover alien life yet. The number of planets that could have the conditions for life on it is in the 100,000s. There are a few explanations draw from this paradox. Maybe there are alien life on other planets but we can not communicate with them. Or Maybe there exist advanced alien civilzations that want to remain hidden from us to observe humans. The last one is that maybe we truly are alone in the universe. To learn more about the Fermi's Paradox check out this website.
